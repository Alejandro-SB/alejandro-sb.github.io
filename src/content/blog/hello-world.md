Welcome to my blog! This is where I'll be writing about software engineering, things I'm learning, and projects I'm working on.

## What to expect

I'll cover topics like:

- **Engineering deep dives** — exploring interesting problems and solutions
- **Tools & workflows** — things that have meaningfully improved how I work
